The Trinity Shamrock
Color and label the three Persons of one God
Name

Directions: Color each leaf of the shamrock a different color. Then trace the name on each leaf. In the center circle, trace "ONE GOD."
God the Father
Try: yellow or gold
The Father is the First Person.
God the Son (Jesus)
Try: red or orange
The Son is the Second Person.
God the Holy Spirit
Try: blue or white
The Spirit is the Third Person.
Trace the Sign of the Cross
In the name of the Father,
and of the Son,
and of the Holy Spirit.
Amen.
Fun Fact: St. Patrick used a shamrock to explain the Trinity — three leaves, one plant! The Trinity is three Persons in one God: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.
