How It Works

Learn. Earn. Collect.

Every faith formation activity on CRUX earns you XP — experience points that build your level and fund your card collection. Spend XP in the Pack Store to open packs and discover which saints you'll receive. The more you study, the more cards you can unlock.

1
Complete Modules
Finish any Faith Formation or Bible Study module to earn XP. Each module rewards based on depth and difficulty.
50–75 XP per module
2
Pass Quizzes
Score higher on quizzes to earn bonus XP. A perfect score on a challenge awards up to 25% extra.
Up to 100 XP per quiz
3
Earn Teacher Awards
Teachers can grant XP awards to students who go above and beyond in class.
Varies by teacher
4
Open Packs
Head to the Pack Store, spend your XP, and discover which saints you've earned — including rare and legendary pulls.
Starting at 200 XP

Rarity Tiers

Not All Saints Are Equal.
In the Pack, Anyway.

Every card in the collection belongs to a rarity tier. Rarer cards have lower pull odds — but they're worth the chase. The most powerful packs give you the best shot at Legendary pulls.

What happens if you pull a card you already own?
Duplicates don't exist in your collection — each saint appears once. If a pack draws a card you already own, it automatically converts to 50 bonus XP instead. Every pull is worth something.
