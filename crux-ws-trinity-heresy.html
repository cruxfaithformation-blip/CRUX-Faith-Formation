Section 1 — Matching

C → Tritheism
D → Macedonianism (Pneumatomachianism)

Section 3 — True or False

1. F — Arius denied the full divinity of Christ.
2. T — The Council of Nicaea convened in 325 AD.
3. F — "Homoousios" means same substance, not similar. "Homoiousios" (with an iota) means similar substance — that was the Arian compromise position.
4. T — Athanasius was exiled five times under different emperors.
5. F — The Trinity is one God in three co-equal Persons, not three gods.
