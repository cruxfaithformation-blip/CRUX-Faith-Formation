Prayer to St. Thomas
O Apostle St. Thomas, you who moved from doubt to the boldest confession of faith — "My Lord and my God" — intercede for all who struggle to believe.
Teach us that honest doubt, brought before the living Christ, becomes the ground of deeper trust. Pray for us in our moments of unbelief, and lead us to the same surrender that transformed your doubt into adoration.
Amen.
Thomas, called Didymus ("the Twin"), was one of the Twelve Apostles. He is remembered for his honest absence at the first resurrection appearance and his subsequent refusal to believe without seeing — and for the moment Christ appeared again and Thomas's doubt became the most complete confession of faith in the Gospels.
Tradition holds that Thomas traveled farther than any other apostle — all the way to India, where he founded seven churches along the Malabar Coast. He was martyred by spear in Mylapore, now Chennai. The Thomas Christians of India trace their lineage directly to him.
