What Is CRUX?

Faith formation that takes
the faith seriously.

CRUX is a Catholic faith formation platform built for students, families, teachers, and seekers. Structured modules, deep scripture study, adult formation, and a gamified card-collecting system that makes learning something students actually want to do.

What's Inside

Everything in one place.

01
Faith Formation

18 modules across five pillars — the Creed, Sacraments, moral life, prayer, and Church identity. Grades 3–8 and Theologian level. Adult formation track included.

02
Bible Study

Guided scripture studies with Greek word studies, Church Fathers, CCC commentary, lectionary ties, and reflection prompts saved to your account.

03
Earn XP

Every module completed, every quiz passed, every challenge finished — all of it earns XP. Your XP opens card packs. Your card packs build your collection.

04
Saint Cards

55 saints across Common, Rare, and Legendary tiers. Holographic flip cards with parallax effect. Open packs, build your deck, collect them all.

05
Practice Games

Six game types — flashcards, word scramble, matching pairs, true/false, fill-in-the-blank, and drag & drop — built for every module.

06
Teacher Tools

Roster management, lesson plans with timestamped discussion guides, class codes, and the ability to award XP directly to students.

The gamified
formation experience.

Complete modules and quizzes to earn XP. Spend XP to open card packs. Build your collection of 55 holographic saint trading cards — Common, Rare, and Legendary.

Finish a faith formation module or pass a quiz. Every interaction earns XP toward your next pack.

XP accumulates in your account. Modules, games, challenges, and teacher awards all count toward your total.
